Banana and Vanilla Cupcakes with Buttercream Frosting

Banana and Vanilla Cupcakes with Buttercream Frosting |They are absolutely delicious. Old-fashioned true cake flavor, texture and density that you just can't get from a box. And the icing is a perfect compliment!

Ingredients

Banana Cupcakes:

  1. 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 1/2 cup butter, at room temperature
  5. 1/2 cup white sugar
  6. 3 eggs, room temperatur
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. 1/4 cup milk
  9. 2 large bananas, chopped

Buttercream Frosting:

  1. 2/3 cup butter, at room temperature
  2. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  3. 2 1/4 cups congectioners' sugar
  4. 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  5. 4 drops yellow food coloring, or as desired
