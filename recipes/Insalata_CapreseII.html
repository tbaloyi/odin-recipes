Insalata Caprese II

Red, white and green. The colours of the Italian flag and of every Italianâ€™s passionate heart. And the colour of the classic Caprese Salad, which originates, as the name suggests, on the Isle of Capri.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
  • fine sea salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  • On a large platter, alternate and overlap the tomato slices, mozzarella cheese slices, and basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil. Season with sea salt and pepper.
